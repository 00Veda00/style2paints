INSTUCTION

Just upload the sketch and reference image, but keep in mind that the reference image should cover as much as possible details in the sketch. The color-picker can help you get any color from your reference image by clicking it. You can add pointed hints to your sketch using "pen".

PREFERENCE

It seems that the AI prefers reference images with white background color. The training dataset is based on anime paints and galgame CGs, thus the AI may be more capable in these field. Remember to chose a right reference image and it is always helpful to achieve better result.
